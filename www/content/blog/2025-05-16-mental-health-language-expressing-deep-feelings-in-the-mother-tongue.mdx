## The Emotional Depth of the Mother Tongue

The mother tongue often serves as an emotional lifeline, embodying a deeper connection to feelings and memories. Studies from the Journal of Neuroscience reveal that bilingual individuals exhibit distinct brain activity patterns when expressing emotions in their mother tongue compared to a second language. This suggests that the mother tongue is intricately linked to emotional memory, making it an authentic vessel for expressing profound feelings. These insights underscore the importance of using one's native language to articulate emotions, offering a more genuine and therapeutic effect.
